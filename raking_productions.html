ABOUT US

We Are Raking Productions

Raking Productions is an independent game development studio and platform founded by RAKING07JANUARY. We believe great games can come from anyone, anywhere.

Our mission is to give indie developers — especially those building with Godot, Unity, and Unreal — a place to share their work and grow an audience without jumping through hoops.

We are also actively developing our own games, tools, and apps under the RP brand — including RP Game Studio, a browser-based creation tool inspired by Roblox.
